Overview

Academic year in CSPI continues from September till June (01.09-30.06). Study duration is different and depends on seeking academic level and type of study. Period of study for Bachelor’s Degree - 4 academic years; for Master's Degree - 2 academic years; Doctoral Degree (PhD or DSc) - 3 (4) academic years, depending on the major (field of study).

We provide full time or part-time academic programs in all fields. International applicants are selected based on face-to-face interview and Admission Committee’s decision.

As a rule, study at CSPI is conducted in Uzbek or Russian, so good preliminary language training is required.

Application procedure and requirements

Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute accepts foreign citizens in accordance with the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of Republic of Uzbekistan №.169 dated on August 4, 2008 and Regulations approved by this Resolution.

Candidates should send and submit the following documents to International Cooperation Department of the Institute:

• Application form for studying at the CSPI;

• The Questionnaire (established sample) for foreign citizen;

• Transcripts from previously completed or in-progress degrees. Please include transcripts/mark sheets and certificates of completion/diplomas of any degrees obtained within and outside the Republic of Uzbekistan:

  • for Bachelor’s Degree: Certificate/Diploma of Secondary Education
  • for Master’s Degree: Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • for Doctoral Degree (PhD, DSc): Master's Degree

• All degrees (certificates of completion/diplomas with transcripts/mark sheets) obtained outside the Republic of Uzbekistan have to be translated and notarized into Uzbek or Russian*;

• An established type of medical certificate, indicating the absence of medical contraindications for study in the Republic of Uzbekistan;

• Certificate, indicating the absence of HIV infection, valid on the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan;

• A current passport or a passport with a residence permit in the Republic of Uzbekistan;

• Six photos measuring 3.5x4.5 cm.

• Abstract and officially certified list of publications (for applicants who want to enroll to the Doctoral programs, internships);

*Note: The translation of transcripts into Russian must be certified by a notarial office in the Republic of Uzbekistan, or by the Embassy or Consulate of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the applicants’ home country.

Higher education in Uzbekistan is not free for students who are coming from abroad. International students are required to pay tuition fees for their studies at CSPI. With the exception of cases stipulated by international, intergovernmental and interdepartmental agreements of the Republic of Uzbekistan, as well as agreements reached by the university with other higher educational institutions of foreign countries.

Based on the interview, we accept:  

  • Foreign citizens who come to study on the basis of international agreements of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  • Citizens of the CIS countries;
  • Other citizens of foreign countries.

Contract conclusion for study

The contract for study in CSPI is formalized after successful passing of the interview.

Getting visa process and registration for housing opportunities

The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Republic of Uzbekistan, upon request of Higher Education Institutions, in agreement with the Ministry of Higher Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, in accordance with the established procedure, ensures the issuance and extension of visas, as well as temporary registration and extension of the temporary residence permit at the place of temporary residence of foreign citizens (according to the available regulations).
