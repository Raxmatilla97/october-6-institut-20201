After  graduating Uzbekistan National University in specialty of teaching  philosophy in 1993, he began his pedagogical career in the department of  ethics and aesthetics of this university. He is a candidate of  philosophy sciences and docent. During his scientific –pedagogical career he was a scientific supervisor of 42 bachelors, 23 masters and a candidate of science. He’s an author of 48 articles, a brochure, 3 methodological guides, 3 e-study guides and 2 textbooks. He’s lecturer of Ethics, Aesthetics, Philosophy of  ethics, Philosophy of elegance, Oratory for bachelors, and Basics of  Ethics and Aesthetics, Etiquette of leading, Theoretical basics of  Ethics and Aesthetics for masters, as well as, Oratory, Oratory of  leader, Oratory and culture of leadership for social organisations. He was a participant of the project of developing  person’s spiritual ethical perfectness and Making up and applying  updated study-methodological base of teaching philosophy in education  system in 2011. And he was the head of the project of National basics of  developing aesthetic upbringing in the process of reforming and  modernizing society that was for 2016-2018. He worked as head of the department, faculty dean,  head of spiritual and enlightenment centre section and pro-rector for  spiritual and enlightenment affairs in Uzbekistan National University.
