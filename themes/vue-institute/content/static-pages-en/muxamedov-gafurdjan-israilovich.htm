He worked as rector of Jizzakh State Pedagogical institute in 1997 and  as rector of Tashkent State Pedagogical University in 2005-2006. He was  rector of Uzbekistan National University in 2006-2015. Since 2017 he has  been a rector of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute.  He was awarded  to “Honoured Scientist of the Republic of Uzbekistan” in 2012. 4  monographs, more than 250 scientific articles in Republican and foreign  journals were published by him. He has 10 certificates for inventions  and discoveries in Chemistry. G. I. Mukhamedov was a scientific  supervisor for 4 doctors of science and 7 candidates of science.
