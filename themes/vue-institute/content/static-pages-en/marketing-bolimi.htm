Marketing service department functions

To establish new educational specialties taking into account the development perspectives of the economy and social sectors of the country and the relevant regions, as well as to analyze proposals for stopping specialty which does not suit to requirements and to submit to pertinent departments of the Ministry.

To study the conquest of the labor market. In order to identify students’ acceptance indicators, to form current and perspective order portfolio for “State grants and contractual agreements” taking into account existing educational trends and requirements to specialists.

To create the database about the new academic year (State grants and acceptance plan of contractual agreements, applications, number and levels of applied students, distribution of specialties, the languages of teaching).

To cooperate with the relevant department (sections) of the Institute (various scientific methodological councils, teaching- methodological sections, faculties, departments, etc.) to improve the professional skills of students in bachelor’s degree and master’s degree and to analyze the effectiveness of measures done by them and to participate in evaluation, to co-ordinate the activities of all relevant units  in marketing departments.

To formalize contracts with companies, institutions, organizations and individuals for the training of specialists.

To establish the commission for the job division of the institute graduates. The Resolution of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan of June 2, 2005 № PQ-92 “On admission to educational Institute of the Republic of Uzbekistan in 2005-2006 academic years”  The graduates of institute are required to work for at least 3 years. To implement on time and control the graduates’ responsibilities for educational institutions, enterprises and organizations. To prepare final distribution of graduates’ work allocation plans according to the real needs of employers’ organizations and enterprises by the Distribution Commission and to draw up and to summarize their personal distribution reports.

To carry out international marketing contracts, to sign contracts with international universities on cooperation. To establish practical training in enterprises and organizations cooperating with foreign countries.

To prepare information on the number of students paying on contractual basis (on the basis of work plan, together with the personnel section).

To establish the database on the basis of studying of the needs of enterprises, institutions and organizations by faculties, chairs and administration, to highly qualified personnel prepared in the institute.

To submit documents of on the organization of the republican database of graduates employment to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education (in coordination with the administration).

To examine and solve applications’ job placement requirements and contractual requirements for students who are sent to work.

To provide with further information on future workplaces, organize job fairs for graduate students.

To prepare information about job placement of graduates of last year.
