Head of the department

 Abdulakhatov Akrom Rozmatovich


The department was established by the order of the rector of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of Tashkent region on October 10, 2019. August 27, 2019 by the decision of the Academic Council of the Chirchik StatePedagogical Institute, A.R.Abdulakhatov was appointed as the head of the department. The teaching staff of the department conducts research works on various topics, as well as teaching relevant subjects.

There are two gyms in the department, there are classes and study halls, the halls are equipped with the necessary sports equipment. The teachers of the department improve students' sports skills in extracurricularsports clubs for active participation of students in city and national competitions.

       

                                     To improve the learning process

The teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies.

 

        The department has established cooperation with the 24th secondary school of Chirchik city of Tashkent region.

 

  

International cooperation:

Today, the department team cooperates with foreign universities and institutes. In particular, it is planned to cooperate with the South Kazakhstan State University named after Mukhtar Avezov in educational, research and research fields.
