   Center for informational technologies (hereinafter the center) is part of the institute and established by the resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dates 23.05.2001 № 230 and order of the Minister of Higher and Secondary Special education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, founded in 2001 by the order №23. The Minister of higher educational system established informational technologies and distance learning centers. “The strategic directions of the Center’s activity are defined by its tasks, the creation and support if computer and information infrastructure of the Institute, the introduction of information technology achievements, and distance learning in the educational process. The head of the Institute’s informational technology is Shukhrat Fakhriddinovich. There are more than 100 computers at the Institute, where all of them are connected to the local network of the Institute. It has access to the Internet with a flexible system for managing and distributing through the local network, which provides full technical and methodological support to the educational process of the Institute with information and communication technologies. Experts of the Center are in the process of setting up and adjusting the mail service.
