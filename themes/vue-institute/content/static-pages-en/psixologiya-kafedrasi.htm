The Department of Psychology, in accordance with the decision of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2019, was separated from the Department of Pedagogy and Psychology as a separate department of Psychology. Professors and teachers of the department teach different subjects and conduct research in different areas.

The Department of Psychology is part of the Faculty of Education and trains specialists in the following areas:


  • Bachelor - "5110900 - Pedagogy and Psychology"

Professors and teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies to improve the educational process.

Students conduct qualification practice in secondary schools of the Tashkent region.


 

The department collaborates with Kiev State University of Ukraine, Moscow State University of Russia, State Institute of Arts and Culture of Uzbekistan, Tashkent State Pedagogical University named after Nizami, the Department of Public Education of the Tashkent region, the Office of Public Education of the city of Chirchik and secondary schools and other organizations
