   Department of Physical Culture was established by order of the Rector of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of Tashkent region on October 10, 2018 № 01-660. August 27, 2019 By the decision of the Academic Council of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute by 1 decision  F.A.Nurmatov was appointed head of the department.

    Professors and teachers of the department teach different subjects and conduct research work in various subjects. The chair of physical culture is a part of the faculty of sports and pre-war military training and trains personnel in the following directions:

 

  •  Bachelor - 5112000–“ Physical culture”
  •  Bachelor - 5111500-“ Pre-conscription military training”
