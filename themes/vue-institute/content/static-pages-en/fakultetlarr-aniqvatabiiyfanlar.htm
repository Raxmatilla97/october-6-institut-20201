The Faculty of Exact Sciences provides vocational training for students in the field of fundamental and applied issues of mathematics, physics and computer science, the use of modern information technologies in professional activities.At the faculty, bachelor's programs are implemented in the following areas: Methods of teaching mathematics, Methods of teaching physics and astronomy, Methods of teaching computer science and master's programs: Methods of teaching the exact sciences (mathematics), Methods of teaching the exact sciences (physics and astronomy). In addition, there is a PhD doctoral program at the faculty.


The teaching staff of the faculty is 74 people, including 8 Doctor of Science and 16 candidates of sciences. Despite the fact that the faculty was recently created, the faculty of the faculty develops new teaching materials, textbooks and laboratory works that are used in the learning process.

Currently, teachers are working with universities and institutions in foreign countries. In particular, there is collaboration with scientists from leading Russian universities, such as the Russian State Pedagogical University, Kazan Federal University, and South Kazakhstan State University named after Mukhtara Avezova, Kiev National University of Ukraine, as well as in the field of teaching and research.

 

At the faculty, great attention is paid to the organization of research activities of students who regularly take part in national and international scientific conferences, competitions, scientific competitions of talented youth.Students of the faculty participate in various sports events, annual contests " Cheers and Smarts (KVN)" and "Student of the Year".The faculty has various circles in the following areas:1. Career and Employment·         Graduates of the faculty work mainly in the education and culture system, research institutes, computer and information centers, as well as in the social sphere

 

·         Also, graduates of the Informatics teaching methodology direction work in information and computing and analytical centers, IT departments of enterprises and organizations, software companies for enterprises in various sectors of the economy, research institutes as database administrators, system and network administrators, developers software, web designers, IT-specialists, programmers for the implementation and maintenance of information products

 

Graduates of the faculty are in great demand among the employers, most of them work in state educational institutions, institutes of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, branches of the Ministry of Defense, higher educational institutions, academic lyceums, professional colleges and production enterprises.
