Together with the launch of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute in Tashkent region, it is necessary to satisfy the intellectual needs of the younger generation, to preserve their cultural and ethical values, to improve the library network, taking into account modern requirements, for the purpose of ensuring implementation of the Resolution of the first President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the organization of information and library provision of the population of the Republic of Uzbekistan" from June 20, 2006, №PP-381 on providing information resource center was established.

The main objectives of the Information Resource Center:

- To assist users with regular education and independent learning using information technology.

- To promote national spiritual-ethical values and to enjoy the cultural and historical heritage of the people, to create opportunities for the creative growth of a rich and harmoniously developed person.

- To provide the necessary information to the population on the basis of new information technologies (electronic databases, Internet resources), widely using any funds through the information requirements and IRC data retrieval system.

- To teach information culture, to train users on modern methods of information search.

- To improve the process based on introduction of modern information technologies and computerization.

- To establish the IRC fund in accordance with Chirchik State pedagogical institute and needs of the users. To create and maintain information search apparatus, databases, catalogs and cartridges.

- To enhance the quality of service, expanding the list of IRC services, technical equipment, and computerization of information processes.

- To study the experience of the developed countries, conducting research and developing the industry.

Electronic library

The electronic library of "Information Resource Center" consists of 1 room. Electronic library of the "Information Resource Center" is equipped with modern computers.

Today, all computers in the "Information Resource Center" electronic library have access to local and global networks and have access to the Internet.

Using the computers available in the Electronic Library of the Information Resource Center, students and professors can use the Internet to get information for themselves and get access to the Tvchdpi, Ziyonet information network.
