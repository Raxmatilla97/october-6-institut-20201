The Educational and Methodical Department is a department that directs and coordinates the educational, teaching and methodological process of the institute, conducts its activities on the basis of laws and other normative acts of the Republic of Uzbekistan, as well as the Charter of the Institute.

The purpose of the Educational and Methodical Department:

  • improvement of the educational process on the basis of advanced scientific achievements, the conduct of its activities on the basis of the State Educational Standards and legal acts which regulating the educational process and the activities of the structural units of the institute;
  • formation of the methodical foundations of the educational process of the institute and the organization of their implementation in practice;
  • ongoing improvement of normative documents on the coordination of educational, methodical work and management of the educational process of faculties and departments of the institute;
  • introduction of new scientific and pedagogical technologies and technical methods into the educational process jointly with other departments of the Institute.

The main tasks of the Educational and Methodical Department:

  • planning, organization, coordination and control of the educational process of the institute;
  • ongoing improvement of regular documents on the coordination and management of educational and methodical work of faculties and departments;
  • summarizing advanced achievements of faculties and departments of the institute in educational and methodical works, and supporting their introduction into the educational process of the institute;
  • making perspective plans and providing suggestions to the Council of the Institute for the development of education;
  • taking control over registration of academic data, student identification card, rating books and other established official  documents;
  • providing practical assistance for faculties and departments in order to improve and update curriculum and programs;
  • taking control over the preparation of documents for making staff schedule of the departments on the basis of existing standards; monitoring the implementation of academic loads.
  • preparation of part-time lecturers’ documents and coordination their fund allocation;
  • monitoring classroom facilities and making a proposal to the Administration on improving their conditions if needed;
  • carrying out all organizational, educational and methodological activities related to the learning process and the activities of students.
