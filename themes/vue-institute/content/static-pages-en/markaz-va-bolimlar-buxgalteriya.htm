Accounting

Accounting section of CSPI is an integral part of the Institute. The department is guided by laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, decrees and resolutions of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, decisions of the Cabinet of Ministers, decisions and orders of the Board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and rector's orders, the regulations of the Institute and the Accounting section.


The accounting section provides the following tasks:


To provide accounting process based on the standard documents approved by the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Uzbekistan;

To control timely calculation and distribution of staff salaries, student scholarships;

To generalize accounting records, to transfer taxes to local and Republican budget in due time;

To spend money from the bank purposefully;

To prevent emergence of debt and indebtedness of creditors;

To control over the proper use of salary fund based on approved staffing charts;

To control over the proper use of the scholarship fund on the orders issued by the rector;

To control over access and use of inventories in accordance with the established rules;

To transfer timely fixed assets, inventories and inventory of funds;

To control over expenditure of budget and off-budget funds based on approved expenditure budgets;

To provide monthly, quarterly, annual reports to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education and other organizations in the prescribed manner.
