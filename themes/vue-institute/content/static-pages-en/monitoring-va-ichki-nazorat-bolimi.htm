Monitoring and internal control section

The department of monitoring and internal control of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of the Tashkent region is carrying out its activity in accordance with the decree of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan of August 2001 № 207, “Internal Monitoring and Monitoring of the Implementation of the National Program for Personnel Training” in accordance with the Charter of the Institute.

The department carries out educational, methodological, spiritual, research, executive discipline, retraining of staff, decisions of the collegium and scientific council and overseeing the implementation statutory documents in the field of education. The Institute implements laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, decisions, orders and other documents of the Ministry, as well as internal orders and decisions of the Institute.

The main tasks of the monitoring and internal control section

Carries out monitoring of implementation of higher education laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, decisions, orders and other documents of the Ministry, as well as internal orders and decisions of the Institute;

Monitoring and monitoring of compliance with state educational standards and other regulatory documents that define the requirements for the quality of training and the level of preparation of the learners;

Analyzing educational process of the faculties, provision of practical and methodological assistance to the units for elimination of identified deficiencies;

Participating in all types of council meetings at the Institute and related educational institutions.
