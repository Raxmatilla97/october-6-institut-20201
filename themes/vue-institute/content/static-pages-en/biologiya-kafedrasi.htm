The department was founded in September 2019 and today the Department of Biology is part of the Faculty of Natural Sciences and is graduating in the following areas:

  • Undergraduate - “Pedagogical Education” (training profile: teaching biology);
  • Master's program - “Pedagogical Education” (training profile: 5A110101-Teaching of the exact and natural sciences (biology));
  • postgraduate study (level of training highly qualified personnel) - "Biological Sciences" (profile: Plant Physiology).


 


Today, a unique multidisciplinary team has formed at the department, in which specialists, scientists and practitioners of various scientific specialties, represented by biological, environmental, pedagogical and agricultural areas, work.

To conduct training sessions there were attracted heads and leading specialists of the organizations and businesses whose activities are related to the direction of ongoing basic professional educational programs. These are specialists in the field of biotechnology, teacher education, ecology and nature management.



To intensify the educational process, the teaching staff of the department uses modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies are used to conduct classes.

Of great importance for consolidating theoretical material and mastering practical skills are practices. During training practices, students master the methodology of field research at natural sites in the Tashkent region.

Creative contacts connect the department with the leading institutions and research institutes of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan: the Research Institute of Genetics and Experimental Plant Biology, the Research Institute of Microbiology, the Research Institute of Zoology, the Center for Genomics and Bioinformatics, the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek, and the Tashkent State Pedagogical Institute Nizami and various educational institutions.

 

The department carries out international cooperation in the implementation of joint projects, scientific internships and the organization of educational practices with several European universities:

 

  • Moscow State University named after Lomonosov (Russia);
  • South Kazakhstan State University named after Mukhtar Avezov (Kazakhstan);
  • Osh State University (Kyrgyzstan);
  • Kazakh National University named after Al-Farabi (Kazakhstan).

 

The department conducts research in the following priority areas:

  • identification, study and characterization of plant viruses in the climatic conditions of Uzbekistan;
  • creation and introduction of cotton varieties stress resistant varieties;
  • creation of biological education on the basis of an innovative educational cluster and increasing the effectiveness of teaching;
  • development and implementation of electronic resources for teaching biology in secondary schools;
  • modern problems of teaching biology;
  • creation of high-yielding and resistant soybean varieties and study of their morpho-physiological properties;
  • the effect of ultrasonic waves on rat hepatocellular cells and the search for correction methods.

 

Teachers of the department regularly train in specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.

 

We invite young people who want to receive a quality education and master fundamental knowledge in the field of biological sciences to train.
