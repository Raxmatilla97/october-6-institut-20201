In accordance with decision No. 1 of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2019, the Department of Music Education and Defectology began to function at the Faculty of Pedagogy.
The department of music education and defectology prepares personnel in the following areas:
• Bachelor - “5111100 - Music Education”;
• Bachelor - “5111900- Defectology (speech therapy)”

 


Teaching disciplines: The direction of music education. School repertoire. Additional tool. Vocals and modern music. Theory of music (solfeggio, harmony, analysis of musical works). Instrumental performance and ensemble. Uzbek folk music. Conducting. Choir and choir studies. Traditional singing. Methods of teaching music and school repertoire. Macom basics. Musical notation and work on computer programs. Music technology training and design. The history of music. Methods of teaching music in preschool institutions. The rhythm. Teaching practice

Defectology:
Clinical basis of children with psycho-physical disorders.
Psycholinguistics. The development of speech in ontogenesis. Special pedagogy and psychology. Speech Therapy. Logo psychology and methods of psychological correction. Habilitation and early diagnosis. Special Speech Development Technique
Psychological and pedagogical diagnostics. Comparative special pedagogy. Speech therapy technology. Logo rhythm. Logomassage. Inclusive education

.                                               

A unique multidisciplinary team was formed at the department, in which specialists, scientists and practitioners of various scientific specialties work. In addition to teaching relevant subjects, teachers of the department carry out research work in various areas.

        As part of the innovation cluster of the Tashkent Regional Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute, the Department of Music Education and Defectology, collaborates with music teachers and speech therapists in the Tashkent region. Conferences, educational-thematic, practical seminars at the regional level are systematically organized. Methodological support and information exchange is carried out in the Telegram social network.
On the basis of a mutually beneficial agreement, the department collaborates with the Olympic Reserves sports specialized boarding school. Students in the field of music education take part in organizing and conducting celebrations at a boarding school.

    In turn, the pupils of the boarding school are regular spectators of the student theater-studio “Nihol” students of musical education.

       Also, as part of the innovation cluster, the department is working in experimental laboratories on the basis of 30 - a specialized multidisciplinary pre-school educational institution for children with hearing impairment, vision and psychophysical development, secondary schools No. 2 and 25 of the city of Chirchik.      

        Under the guidance of teachers of the department, laboratories conduct correctional-developing, entertaining and musical classes.


Students of musical education are participants and prize-winners of national and international competitions and festivals. For example, in June this year, a group of folk instrument performers won first places in the competition “Musical season, summer 2019” organized by the center of creative initiatives “ART-ASPEKT” at the Kazakhstan National University of the Arts.

 

The international cooperation-         The teaching staff of the department strives to keep abreast of modern innovations in the field of music education and defectology. In this regard, it cooperates with a number of universities in Russia, Kazakhstan and Belarus. These are:-         - Moscow State Pedagogical Institute named after Gnesin, Russia. In the person of the national artist of Russia, professor, conductor, head of the department - Voronin Boris Sergeevich.-         - East Siberian Academy of Culture and Art. Negotiations are underway on the participation of students in the field of music education in the international competition "Baikal Cup" in Ulan-Ude, the exchange of experience of teachers in the academy.-         - Institute of defectology. Tanka. Republic of Belarus. Negotiations are ongoing on exchange visits between teachers and students.-         - Center for Creative Initiatives "Art Aspect" at the Kazakh National University of Arts. Astana city. Negotiations are underway on the participation of a group of students in the final stage of the folk musical instrument contest, which will be held in March 2020, in Rome, Italy. Also, our teachers of the department took a course of increasing qualifications in courses of the FPK at the Center for Creative Initiatives "Art Aspect" at the Kazakh National University of Arts.-         Students of musical education and defectology are actively involved in the socio-cultural life of not only the institute, but also in the city of Chirchik, as well as in the Tashkent region. In just one year, students performed in more than 100 events. These are concerts of chamber, traditional and pop music, more than 20 shows of the play "Or tepa", children's musical fairy tales,-         KVN club, charity concerts. Our audience: children of kindergartens, secondary schools, specialized schools and schools of music and arts, children of boarding schools Mehribonlik, cadets of the tank school in Chirchik, vacationers at the Buston sanatorium, music teachers and speech therapists of the region, and many others.
