In accordance with decision No. 1 of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2019, the Department of History began to function at the Department of History and languages Sciences, having separated from the Department of " History and social Sciences". The Department of History is part of the Faculty of History and languages Sciences and prepares personnel in the following areas:

 

  • Bachelor - “5110600 - Methods of teaching history”;

 

Readable disciplines:

  1.  World history
  2.  Auxiliary History Sciences
  3.  History of Uzbekistan
  4.  Archeology
  5.  Ethnology
  6.  Introduction of modern educational tools in teaching history
  7.  Historical Geography
  8.  Methods of teaching history, personality and society
  9.  History of World Civilizations
  10.  Methods of teaching history
  11. Historiography
  12.  Historical local lore and tourism
  13.  Ethnogenesis and ethnic history of the Uzbek people
  14.  Archives
  15.  History of Uzbek culture
  16.  Technology and design in teaching history
  17.  Geography of ancient written monuments of Central Asia
  18.  History of Uzbekistan (for all directions)
  19.  Religious Studies (for all majors)

 

A unique multidisciplinary team was formed at the department, in which specialists, scientists and practitioners of various scientific specialties work. In addition to teaching relevant subjects, teachers of the department carry out research work in various areas.

In addition to the main teachers of the department, specialists from research institutes of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan and Professor-teachers from leading universities were involved in the training.

At the department there are two modernly equipped laboratory rooms where students conduct laboratory work, the rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment and reagents.

Young researchers of the department the role of clusters in the teaching of historical science, Pilgrimage tourism in Uzbekistan Pamir expeditions, research works.

To improve the educational process, teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies.


   

Students conduct practical training at the Institute of History of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, at the places of cultural heritage, archives, museums of Tashkent region and the city of Tashkent and in secondary schools of the Tashkent region.

The department collaborates with the Institute of History of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, State Museum of History of Uzbekistan, many schools in Chirchik with the aim of developing a school lab, Mirzo Ulugbek National University of Uzbekistan and other institutions.

 

International cooperation: today the department team collaborates with foreign universities and institutes. In particular, it collaborates with scientists and researchers from leading higher educational institutions, such as the South Kazakhstan State University, Mukhtar Avezov.

Teachers of the department regularly take advanced training courses at specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.

 

We invite young people who want to receive a quality education and master fundamental knowledge in the field of historial sciences to train.
