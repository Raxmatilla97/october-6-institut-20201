Information about the faculty

The faculty of history and languages was established based on the decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan under the decree PD-3152 in June 27, 2017. Since it was found spiritual and educational affairs have been constituted as one of faculties in the institute.

From the early academic year, 2017-2018, 232 students for 6 educational directions were accepted to the faculty.

In the beginning period of the faculty there were 2 departments, but now it includes 4 departments such as Uzbek language and literature, history and social sciences, foreign languages, Russian language and literature.

Study process affairs

In the department of Uzbek language and literature 11 teachers have been working on the main staff and one of them is doctor of science in philology and professor, and 4 of them are candidate of science, one of them is senior teacher, 5 of them are teachers, and 3 of doctor of sciences are working as exterior staff, two of them are hourly working teachers.

In the department of History and social sciences 16 teachers are working on the main staff. 2 of them are candidate of science, 2 of them are senior teachers, 12 of them are teachers. 10 candidates of science and teachers are working as exterior staff, and the department has an hourly working teacher.

In the department of foreign languages 11 teachers are working on the main staff. One of them is candidate of science. The department has an exterior staff teacher.

In the department of Russian language and literature there are 11 teachers, and one of them is candidate of science.

Nowadays 553 students are studying in the faculty that has 6 educational directions such as 5111400-foreign language and literature (English), 5110600-methodology of teaching History, 5111600-national notion, spiritual basics and education of Law, 5111200-Uzbek language and literature, 5111301-Russian language and literature, 5111306-Russian language and literature (in other language groups).

At the present time Yosh tarixchilar, Ma’anviyat gulshani, Yosh targ‘ibotchilar, Любители русского языка, Русское слово, Matnshunos, Iste’dod, Super skills of English, Funny English extra-curricular activities are being conducted for students for the reason that they manage their free time correctly. More than 350 students are enrolled in these activities. More than 3 languages are being taught without charge.

Additionally, special sports and language learning courses have been organized for the students who live in students’ accommodation of the institute.

Scientific affairs

Scientific forums and seminar are constantly held in the faculty and teachers actively take part in them with their scientific researches. For instance, more than 20 articles, 88 theses and a learning guide have been written and 18 articles were prepared for the print. According to the scientific plan of the faculty scientific forums and seminar are regularly organized in the departments. 5 independent researchers have their scientific research works.

Working with skilled students were systematically established. Dialogues, that are for their working on translation of fictional stories and poems, developing their practice of speech, developing to write scientific and journal articles, were set up. Students are doing scientific and practical researches going to the sacred and historical places of Tashkent region. By selections students have specially enrolled in extra-curricular activities based on their interests.

Spiritual-enlightenment affairs

In order to further improve political and legal knowledge of students of the faculty, as well as, make them absorb political and social processes that are going round, information about speeches of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, interviews of republican statesmen and different events happening in the country is regularly given in hours of information and curators co-operating with the department of working with the Youth of the institute. Furthermore, lots of broadly planned works are being done to make the students comprehend the essence of reforms on spiritual-enlightenment, socio-politics and economics in our country, discuss them among students.

On the basis of the plan of faculty science parties are held for birthdays of great statesmen, authors who have big signs in the history of Uzbek nation. Students of the faculty have been taking high places from different competitions between institute, district, city, Republic, international and high education apartments. Our teachers and students have been taking 1st, 2nd and 3rd places in sports competitions such as “Cup of the institute”, “Cup of the faculty’ in football among institute staff.
