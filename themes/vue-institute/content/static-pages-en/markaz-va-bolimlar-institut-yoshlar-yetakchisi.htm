The purpose of the primary organization

* Support for youth initiatives;

* Identify talented and gifted among young people;

* Comprehensively promoting the protection of the rights and legitimate interests of young people;

* Assistance in solving social problems of youth


The main tasks of the primary organization

* Study and analysis of the level of mastering students of subjects, the quality of education, the use of textbooks and libraries;

* Assistance in the consideration and resolution of appeals of young people;

* Implementation of measures aimed at deepening the knowledge of young people, learning foreign languages and their mastery of modern information and communication technologies and their professional orientation.


Benefits provided by the Union of Youth of Uzbekistan

* For students with a recommendation from military service, with an average mastering rate of 71 percent or more, 35 percent of the amount of a paid education contract is paid from the funds of the Youth Union Development Fund of Uzbekistan

* With an average mastering rate of 86 percent and above, 35 percent of the amount of a paid education contract is paid from the funds of the Youth Development Fund of Uzbekistan

* In order to encourage young people who have achieved high results and achievements in various fields and industries, the Mard Uglon State Prize has been established

* In order to implement the state youth policy, to encourage young people who actively participate in studies, work and social work, the “Kelajak Bunyodkori” Medal was instituted
