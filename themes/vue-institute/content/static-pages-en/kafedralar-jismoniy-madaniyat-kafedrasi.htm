Department of "Physical Culture" was established by order 01-660 of the rector of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of Tashkent region in October 10, 2018.

At the department there are the following bachelor educational directions - 5112000 - Physical Culture and Sports (including Women's Sports).

The following specialty subjects have been taught in the department under the educational direction Physical culture and Sports: History of physical culture, theory and methodology of physical culture, methodology of sport and leisure games, teaching methods of gymnastics and its training, sports skills, physical education hygiene and theoretical and practical lessons on the basics of healthy lifestyle, including the medical basics of sports as the science of choice.

At the department Sports facilities, Methodology of Sport and Apprenticeship Games, Sports competitions, Theoretical and practical lessons on tourism are taught as the science of conducting and debating for three-year special correspondence, secondary and post-graduate students of the Department of Physical Culture.

At the department there are extra-curricular activities such as "Volleyball", "Football", "Table Tennis", "Kurash", "Chess - -Draughts" and "Theory and technique of physical culture" as scientific circles.

Teachers of the Department have been carrying out scientific research on socio-political, economic and cultural life of athletes in Uzbekistan as well as international sports competitions have been held in Tashkent during the years of independence: Worldwide Forms and Methods of Physical Culture Development in the Process of Out-of-Class Students' Training, Giving Growth on the Basis of Continuous Education - Scientific Basis of Physical Education, Methods of Eliminating Physical Problems of Preschool Children, the ways of making students absorb our national values through Belt Wrestling Kurash.
