INFORMATION ABOUT SCIENTIFIC ACTIVITIES

   

Under the initiative of the rector of the institute, two major scientific research groups were established in the Institute for scientific research.

   

  •     
  • Scientific research group on exact specialties (The group is mainly engaged in research on the physicochemical properties and application of functional polymer materials with new properties on the basis of local raw materials).
  •    
  • Scientific research group on pedagogy (scientific research on pedagogical modern teaching and pedagogical technologies is being carried out).
