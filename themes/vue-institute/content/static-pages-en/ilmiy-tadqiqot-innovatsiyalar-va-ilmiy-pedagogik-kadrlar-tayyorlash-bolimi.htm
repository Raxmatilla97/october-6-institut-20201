Goals and tasks of the section


The Laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan “On Education” and “On the National Program of Personnel Training”, the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan №PP-2789 of February 2, 2017 “On measures to further improve the organization, management and financing of business” in Tashkent Region Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute  in the activity of the Department of scientific and scientific pedagogical cadres, the Constitution of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Decrees of the Chambers of the Oliy Majlis, Decrees and Orders of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Resolutions and Orders of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Decrees and Orders of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Higher Attestation Commission under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan as well as the decisions of the Tashkent Region Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute Council and the Council of Tashkent Region Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute as well as the implementation of the orders of the rector of the Tashkent Region Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute, the development of fundamental, applied and innovative researches at the university, the implementation of state scientific and technical programs and science and technology priorities, and coordination of innovation cooperation between production, organization of research on priority, perspective and advanced scientific directions.


• Establishment of annual and perspective plans of scientific research works, organization of their implementation, provision of appropriate methodological assistance, preparation of reports based on established procedures in the faculties and departments, scientific laboratories and departments.


• Admission to doctorate (DSc) and base doctorate (PhD), development and implementation of academic staffing strategy of faculties and departments.


• Involvement of university scholars on foreign and state grants, on economic contracts, ensuring the active participation of Chairs in the development of scientific developments in the national economy, innovative cooperation and fairs.


• Development and implementation of international, republican and local scientific conferences and seminars with the participation of professors and teachers at the university.


• Coordinate the activities of the student's scientific circles, young scientists and student societies in the faculties and departments, and organize their effective functioning. Coordinate the involvement of young scientists and gifted students in fundraising activities.


• Development and implementation of the publication of monographs, articles and conference materials on the results of scientific research.


• Monitoring of the work carried out in the departments “Innovative corporate partnership of science, education and production”.
