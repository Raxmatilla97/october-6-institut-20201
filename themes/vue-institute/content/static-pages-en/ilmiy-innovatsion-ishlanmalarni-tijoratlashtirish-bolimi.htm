The section of commercializing scientific-innovative complexes

Activity of the section of commercializing scientific and innovative complexes


The section of commercializing scientific-innovative complexes was established in accordance with the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan № 3698 dated May 7, 2018 "On additional measures to improve the implementation of mechanisms which belong to innovations in economical spheres". Activity of the section is based on the laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the orders of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, other governmental bodies of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and protection of intellectual property, international agreements, orders of the rector of the institute on the basis of their regulation and other normative documents.


The section of commercializing complexes was established in order to ensure the commercializing scientific and innovative complexes and to provide with commercializing the results of the research activity, which concern institute's right, and it to analyze market and to study the need for innovative products (works, services), to evaluate profitability that belongs to commercializing innovative products, to sort projects which are ready for commercialization and applying, to attract investors, partners and interested people for carrying projects, to establish new technologies and to collaborate with industrial enterprises on producing innovative products.


The section of commercialization carries out the following activities:


To attract professors, teachers, assistants and other personnel, doctoral students, students, as well as specialists from outside organizations in the research work of the Institute, funded from the budget and off-budget sources, and services provided by higher education institutions to customers;

The institute carries out preparing demands for patenting, formalizing and presenting and recording objects of intellectual property in the Republic of Uzbekistan and in foreign countries.

To prepare and provide contracts with the authors of objects of intellectual property envisaged by the legislation and controls their execution;

To carry out work on the registration of the right to the objects of intellectual property together with other section of the Institute;

To carry out the work complexes created by the institute scientists and results of IRA (innovative research activity) about collecting, storing and forming of data, to use the results of activity effectively;

To organize expertise, analysis and evaluation of the results of IRA and innovative projects with scientific, technical, scientific-methodological and innovative capabilities, and to identify the most important and competitive complexes;

To carry out work out of references for managing intellectual property by containing distribution and transfer of rights to the results of IRA in all steps of innovative cycle;

To show consultative services in the sphere of transferring technologies, for preparing business plans and innovative projects, for providing legal protection of IRA results, for searching potential investors;

To determine the facts about illegally using the institute objects of intellectual property, and give suggestions, that eliminate infringement of right and provide compensation of damage, for taking actions by administration;

To promote the protection of the Institute’s property rights to intellectual property, as well as the protection of property and personal non-property rights of authors;;

To organize seminars for intellectual property professionals for institute staff;

To prepare and implement proposals for innovation and patent-licensing activities, innovation entrepreneurship and technology transfer in the budget project of the Institute;

To work out documents on issues related to creation, protection and use of intellectual property objects.
