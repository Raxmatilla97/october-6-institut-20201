Department of Primary Education In accordance with the decision of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2019, the Department of Preschool and Primary Education was separated from the Department of Preschool and Primary Education. The Department became independent and began to function. The faculty and teachers of the department also teach different subjects and topics and conduct research.

The Department of Primary Education is part of the Faculty of Preschool and Primary Education and provides training in the following areas::

 

  • • Undergraduate - "5111700 - Primary Education and Sports"

In addition to the main teachers of the department, experts from research institutes and professors from leading universities participate in the transfer of knowledge.

 

 

Professors and teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies to improve the educational process.

Qualification practice is conducted by students of secondary schools in the Tashkent region.

The department cooperates with the Scientific Research Institute of Pedagogical Sciences of Uzbekistan named after Kori Niyozi, Tashkent State Pedagogical Institute named after Nizami, the Department of Public Education of the Tashkent region, the Department of Public Education of the city of Chirchik, secondary schools and other organizations.
