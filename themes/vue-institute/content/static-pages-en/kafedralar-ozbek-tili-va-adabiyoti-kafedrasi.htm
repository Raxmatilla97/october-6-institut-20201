In accordance with decision No. 1 of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2018, the Department of Uzbek Language and Literature began to function at the Faculty of History and Languages, having separated from the Department of Languages.

The Department of Uzbek Language and Literature is part of the Faculty of History and Languages and prepares personnel in the following areas:


• Bachelor - 5111200 - Uzbek language and literature;

• Bachelor - 5111300 - Native language and literature (Tajik language and literature);

• Bachelor - 5111300 - Native language and literature (Kazakh language and literature).

To improve the educational process, teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies.

Students conduct professional practice at the Institute of the Uzbek language literature and folklore of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the State Museum of Literature named after Alisher Navoi.

The department collaborates with the Institute of Uzbek Language, Literature and Folklore of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the University of Uzbek Language and Literature named after Alisher Navoi, the State Museum of Literature named after Alisher Navoi, the Department of Public Education of the Tashkent Region, and the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek.

International cooperation: nowadays, the department plans on cooperation with foreign universities and institutes
