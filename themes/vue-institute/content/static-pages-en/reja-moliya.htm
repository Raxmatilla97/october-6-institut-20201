About activities of the department

Finance planning department is a sub-structural unit of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute. Main duties of the department include development of prospective and current plans, forecasting expenditures for supporting units of institute, determining need for funding the institution from different sources (state budget, non-budget activities, incomes from scientific-research works and others). The department deals with the compilation of income and expenditure for all types of activities, analyzes the financial and economic activities of the institute, and participates in drafting of prospective projects and annual plans for admission of students. Moreover, it also monitors the correctness of assigning educational grants, attends in development of provisions on educational grants, internal regulations, the charter of the university, etc.

Main responsibilities and functions of the department are:

  • creating and developing staff schedule;
  • developing the forecast of incomes and expenses, preparing and controlling budget of expenses and incomes;
  • conducting comprehensive economic analysis of all activities of the institute;
  • conducting statistic data in all economic indicators of work, preparing periodic reports in a timely manner and the systematizing analytical materials;
  • development of regulatory framework for the remuneration of teaching, training, supporting, and administrative staff for the new academic year;
  • adjustment of the staff schedule in compliance with changes in the regulatory framework;
  • accounting for sources of funding;
  • preparation of budgets for planned expenditures and incomes for budgetary and extra-budgetary funding, analysis of the budget expenditure and salary expenditure quarterly basis;
  • adjustment of the budget for expenditures and revenues after approval of the limits for the budget financing of the institute by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education and the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  • monitoring the allocation of funds for salary on budgetary and extra budgetary financing by categories of employees and divisions;
  • assigning salary for newly recruited employees and annual auditing the staff schedule.
