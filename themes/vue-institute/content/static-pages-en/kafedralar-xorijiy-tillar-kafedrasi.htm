The Department was established in September 2018 and today is a structural unit of the faculty of History and languages and trains specialists in the following areas:

  • Bachelor - "Pedagogical education" (preparation: foreign language and literature (English));
  • bachelor's degree - "Pedagogical education" (preparation: foreign language and literature (German));
  • master's degree - "Pedagogical education" (direction of training: foreign language and literature (English)).

To organize the educational process in an interesting way, managers and leading specialists of personnel and other production enterprises and organizations involved in it are involved in the implementation of the training results into practice. These specialists are the leading specialists in the field of pedagogical education, teaching methods and innovative teaching techniques.

To activate the educational process, the Institute teachers support modern pedagogical technologies, multimedia and information and communication technologies in the educational process.


There are school practices that reinforce theoretical knowledge and are closely correlated with practice. Students will gain knowledge and skills about the methodology of teaching foreign languages and teaching activities during school practice.

The Department works in cooperation with a number of organizations listed below to promote research and creative work. They can be admitted to the national University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek, TSPU named after Nizami and secondary schools.

The Department has established international cooperation with universities of several European and CIS countries in order to prepare joint projects, organize scientific internships and educational practices.

The Department conducts research in the following priority areas:

design technology of English language teaching in higher educational institutions;

didactic foundations of fiction in German language teaching;

the formation of a culture of intercultural communication in teaching English to high school students;

features of new gender terms in linguistics.

We invite applicants for bachelor's degree and students with a bachelor's degree in foreign language for quality education and fundamental knowledge in teaching a foreign language.
