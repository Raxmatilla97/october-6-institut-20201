The department was formed as a result of the fact that, in 2019, the faculty of History and Languages from the department of History and Social Sciences was highlighted as Social Sciences. The department became known as the “Social Sciences” on the basis of the 1st decree of the scientific council of August 27, 2019, as well as the order of the rector of 2019 on September 4. The Department of Social Sciences has 1 professor and 8 candidates of sciences. For full-time, as well as for correspondence courses on the part of the faculty of the department during classes, interactive methods, innovative, information and pedagogical technologies are used. The Department of Social Sciences is part of the Faculty of History and Language Disciplines and prepares personnel in the following areas:

In addition to the main teachers of the department, professors and teachers of leading higher educational institutions were involved in training.

 

To improve the educational process, the teaching staff of the department uses modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies.

Students conduct practical training in educational institutions of the Tashkent region.

In school number 21 of the city of Chirchik, the “School Laboratory” of the department was created. Partnerships have been established with the academic lyceum at the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute and other organizations of the Tashkent region.


International cooperation: at present the staff of the department is working together with foreign universities and institutes. In particular, cooperation has been established in the educational, methodological, and research fields with scientists from leading higher educational institutions. Including with such a department as “Philosophy and Cultural Studies” of the South Kazakhstan State University named after Mukhtar Avezov.

Teachers of the department regularly improve their qualifications in specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.

We invite interested persons to study in the field of ideas and or law.
