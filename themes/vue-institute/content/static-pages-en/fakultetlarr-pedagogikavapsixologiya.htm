The faculty of Pedagogics has been reorganized at the Chirchik state pedagogical institute of Tashkent region since 2019-2020 academic year.

There are three chairs in the education-methodical, scientific, spiritual and educational process of the faculty:

Pedagogics and management;

Psychology;

Musical Education and Defectology.

There are 48 professors and teachers, including 2 doctors and professors, 8 candidates and professors at the faculty. Totally 1074 students study at the Faculty of pedagogics in the direction of Bachelor's degree. 497 of them get education in the form of daytime education, 310 of them get education in the form of remote learning, 179 get education in the form of special remote learning and 88 of them get second higher education.
