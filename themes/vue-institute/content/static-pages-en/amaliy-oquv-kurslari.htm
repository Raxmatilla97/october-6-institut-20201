As the framework signed agreements or exchange programs, foreign students or researchers have opportunity to involve in internship courses at the CSPI.

Candidates should send following documents:

An official representation signed by the Head of International Department of the partner university. Until we receive the original, we ask to send this letter via fax +998 (70) 712-45-41 or scanned form via e-mail to intercoop@cspi.uz

We also ask to send completed application form via fax or e-mail.        

A study or research plan containing a briefly explained statement of purpose and its expected results.

After reviewing documents and dealing with the host faculty, you will be notified of further required steps.

Please note that all foreign citizens must be registered at the staying place within three working days after their arrival to the territory of Uzbekistan. Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute can assist in registering arriving candidates only if they:

• entered the territory of Uzbekistan on a visa issued on the basis of invitation from CSPI;

• students who are living at dormitories of  the institute. Living on campus may also possible only in the case of a visa on the basis of invitation from CSPI.
