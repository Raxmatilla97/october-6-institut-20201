Talented students of Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute

A positive tradition of supporting and encouraging talented students is being developed in Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute. The opportunities have been created such as attracting talented students to the scientific and creative environment of the institute, their involvement in scientific Olympiads, various competitions, scientific projects that carried out on the basis of the State Program, and research work, conferences and seminars, student scientific societies and design bureaus, as well as publishing scientific and public articles, stories and poems in the publications and conducting independent creative researches.

Students of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute are actively participating in the Republican Olympiads every year.
