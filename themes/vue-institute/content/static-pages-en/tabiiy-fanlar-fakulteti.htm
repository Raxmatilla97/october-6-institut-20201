Faculty of Natural Sciences at Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute, Tashkent region On September 3, 2019 the faculty of natural sciences became independent faculty. In the educational, methodical, scientific, spiritual and educational process of the faculty actively involved in the production of 3 specialists:

  • Chemistry;
  • Biology;
  • Geografy;

532 full-time, 54 evening, 85 part-time and 15 second-year correspondence students study in the modern equipped classrooms and laboratories of the Faculty of Natural Sciences. The educational building of the faculty is designed for 900 seats and has a room for chemistry, biology, physics laboratories and geography smart room. In the 2019-2020 academic year, 22 masters were enrolled in 3 specialties.

The faculty has reached agreements with several foreign countries with universities in Europe and Asia on international grants, research and teaching practices. Including:

  • Moscow State University (Russia);
  • Southern Federal University of Russia;
  • South Kazakhstan University named after Mukhtar Avezov
  • Osh State University (Kyrgyzstan)
  • Tajikistan state pedagogical university named after S. Ayniy
  • University of Humboldt (Germany)


The staff of the faculty cooperates with the research institutes of the Republic of Uzbekistan, reputable higher education institutions of the Republic and secondary schools of Tashkent region. To them

  •  Institute of General and Inorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  •  Institute of Plant Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  •  Institute of Biororganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  •  Institute of Genetics and Plant Biology of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  •  National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek;
  •  Tashkent State Pedagogical University named after Nizami;
  •  JSC “MAXAM-CHIRCHIQ” in Chirchik;
  •  Tashkent Botanical Garden and others.

The faculty and students of the faculty are actively involved in scientific grants, various contests, Olympiads, exhibitions and honorary places.

Doctor of Pedagogical Sciences, Professor A.K.Rakhimov "Improvement of biology and methods of teaching in the Republic", candidate of biological sciences, associate professor V.B.Fayziev "Detection, characterization and diagnosis of phytopathogenic viruses in the climatic conditions of Uzbekistan and development of measures for them", doctor of Biological Sciences, B.Amanov, "Creation and implementation of high-yielding lines and varieties of cotton resistant to various stresses", doctor of Chemical Sciences, Associate Professor D.J.Bekchanov "Study of the synthesis of Ionites and their physical and chemical properties on the basis of local raw materials", doctor of Biological Sciences, Professor R.A.Eshchanov "Drought-tolerant varieties of plants with the preservation of biologically active substances", candidate of geographical sciences, associate professor R.Y.Maxamadaliev "Problems of economic and social development of rural areas", doctor of philosophy F.S.Rajabov conducts research on the directions of "Improving the territorial organization of livestock industries in Uzbekistan" and other professors and teachers carry out research work on the priority directions of the department.

At the faculty 68 teachers, including 9 professors, 22 candidates of sciences and senior lecturers are involved in the educational process.

Professors and teachers of the faculty have created more than 40 educational-methodical and scientific literature, more than 8 laboratory works and are used in the educational process.

As a result of the scientific activity of the faculty, 8 patents, 2 copyright certificates and 1 practical grant worth 400 million soums were obtained.
