      In accordance with decision No. 1 of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 30, 2019, the Department of Physics began to function at the Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences.                                                  

The Department of Physics prepares personnel in the following areas:


  •  Bachelor - “5110200 - Methods of Teaching Physics and Astronomy
  •  Master's program - “5A110201 - Methods of teaching exact and natural sciences (Physics)”

 

Teaching disciplines:

General physics

Physics Teaching Methodology

Astronomy course

Methodology of teaching astronomy

Introducing computer animation of a physical phenomenon

Theoretical physics

Electrical Engineering

Technology for solving problems in physics and astronomy

Selected Chapters of Elementary Physics

A unique multidisciplinary team was formed at the department, in which specialists, scientists and practitioners of various scientific specialties work. In addition to teaching relevant subjects, teachers of the department carry out research work in various areas.

Leading teachers of the department:

• Mukhamedov Gofurjon Isroilovich - Doctor of Chemical Sciences, Professor. Leading specialist in the field of composite materials

• Tursunov Ikromjon Gulomjonovich - doctor of physical and mathematical sciences, specialist in the field of semiconductor physics

• Usmanov Shukrullo Nematovich - Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, specialist in the field of semiconductor physics

• Rustamov Umid Rakhmatovich - candidate of physical and mathematical sciences, specialist in the field of magnetooptics


In addition to the main teachers of the department, specialists from research institutes and professors from leading universities of the Academy of Sciences of Uzbekistan are involved in training.

There are two laboratories at the department, where students conduct classrooms, rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment and laboratory equipment. Young researchers of the department carry out research work in laboratories. Professors and teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies to improve the educational process.

Practical classes are conducted by students at the Physicotechnical Institute of the Academy of Sciences of Uzbekistan, at the Astronomical Institute and in secondary schools of the Tashkent region. The department collaborates with the Physicotechnical Institute of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Astronomical Institute, the Ion-Plasma Institute, the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek and other organizations. 

 


International cooperation: today, employees of the department collaborate with foreign universities and institutes. In particular, collaborates with scientists from leading universities in the world, such as the Russian State Pedagogical University. A.I. Gersen, South Kazakhstan State University named after Mukhtara Avezova, Kiev National University in Ukraine.

Teachers of the department regularly undergo training at specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.

         We invite young people who want to get quality and fundamental knowledge in physics.
