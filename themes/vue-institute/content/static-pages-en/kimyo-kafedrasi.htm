In accordance with decision No. 1 of the Council of the Chirchik State Pedagogical Institute of August 27, 2019, the Department of Chemistry began to function at the Department of Natural Sciences, having separated from the Department of "Natural Sciences". The Faculty of Chemistry is part of the Faculty of Natural Sciences and prepares personnel in the following areas:

 

  • Bachelor - “5110300 - Methods of teaching chemistry”;
  •  Master - “5A110301 - Methods of teaching the exact and natural sciences (Chemistry)”;

A unique multidisciplinary team was formed at the department, in which specialists, scientists and practitioners of various scientific specialties work. In addition to teaching relevant subjects, teachers of the department carry out research work in various areas.

In addition to the main teachers of the department, specialists from research institutes of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan and teachers of NUUz were involved in the training.


At the department there are two modernly equipped laboratory rooms where students conduct laboratory work, the rooms are equipped with the necessary equipment and reagents.

Young researchers of the department carry out research work on obtaining polymer sorbents based on local raw materials and the study of their physico-chemical properties.

To improve the educational process, teachers of the department use modern teaching methods, multimedia tools and information and communication technologies.


Students conduct practical training at the Institute of Plant Chemistry and Bioorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and in secondary schools of the Tashkent region.

The department collaborates with the Institute of Plant Chemistry, the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry, the Institute of Chemistry and Physics of Polymers, the Institute of General and Inorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Maxam-Chirchik JSC, Mirzo Ulugbek National University of Uzbekistan and other institutions.

 


International cooperation: today the department team collaborates with foreign universities and institutes. In particular, it collaborates with scientists and researchers from leading higher educational institutions, such as the Russian State Pedagogical University named after A.I. Herzen, South Kazakhstan State University, Mukhtar Avezov and Kiev National University of Ukraine.

Teachers of the department regularly take advanced training courses at specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.

 

We invite young people who want to receive a quality education and master fundamental knowledge in the field of natural sciences to train.
