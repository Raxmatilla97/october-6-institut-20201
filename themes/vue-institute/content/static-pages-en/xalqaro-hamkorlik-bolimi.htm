Department responsibilities:

  • promoting the development of international relations;
  • coordination of the institute’s departments in the field of international cooperation;
  • coordination, documentary support and assistance in the implementation of international cooperation projects;
  • providing information and consulting services in the field of international cooperation;
  • language support for correspondence between institute units and events.

Major dimensions:

  • Academic mobility
  • organization of internships in the framework of student exchange programs for students of CSPI in partner universities and for foreign students in CSPI;
  • advising students on academic mobility programs.

2. Foreign citizens or international students

• systematization of information about foreign students studying at the institute;

• supporting in visa getting and residency process for foreign students, prepare invitations for participants in conferences, etc.

3. Establishment international collaboration and preparation of MOU and agreements with foreign HEIs.

5. Analyze data and statistics on international relations and international activities.

6. Language support correspondence (within the framework of the activities of the international department and at the request of structural units).

7. Doing practical training (English, translation and interpretation) on the basis of the department with students studying in “Foreign language and literature: English” and “Foreign language and literature: German” majors.

8. Organize the meetings and briefings with foreign delegations in the several levels.
