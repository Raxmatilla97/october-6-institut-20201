The strategy of the faculty

The most important activity of the faculty of sports and military conscription education is to improve the educational, professional and cultural level of students, modernization of the material and technical base, strengthening international relations, forming business ties with educational institutions, the media, press services and other partners. At the same time, involving the General population in sports activities and maintaining a healthy lifestyle; creating modern conditions for young people to regularly engage in physical culture and sports; strengthening of confidence in the forces and opportunities through sports in the youth environment, formation of feeling of patriotism at younger generation; identification and selection of young people for sports.

Strategic objectives of the faculty of sports and conscription military education-the formation of scientific schools that carry out research in the field of sports, physical culture and physical culture, as well as military conscription education; training of highly qualified teachers who have mastered the technology of theoretical and practical training in the field of sports and conscription military education; attracting highly qualified domestic and foreign experts in the field of military sports education.; development of modern training programs and innovative technologies aimed at training professional athletes; conducting in-depth research aimed at the introduction of innovative technologies at all levels of training and education; strengthening and comprehensive improvement of the existing educational, methodological, material and technical base, pedagogical technologies that provide a qualitatively new stage of training athletes, the introduction of modern technical means in the educational process; increasing the level of theoretical and practical knowledge of students, the formation of their professional competencies.

Our duty is to form a civic position, spiritual qualities, a sense of responsibility, independence in decision-making, initiative, tolerance, the ability to successfully socialize in society, the formation of worldview and culture of the individual, which has the qualities of maintaining and strengthening the health of athletes and public organizations in various forms, means, not only in sports activities, but also in everyday life.

History and structure of the faculty

On August 27, 2019, the faculty of sports and conscription military education was established as part of the Chirchik state pedagogical Institute of Tashkent region.

This position was appointed Dean of the faculty associate Professor Telman Sadullaevich Achilov.

Currently, the faculty has 4 departments. In particular, physical culture, interfacial physical education, technical education, fine arts and engineering graphics.

The teaching staff of the faculty: presently the faculty has 45 professors and teachers.

Practice and employment

In the 2019-2020 academic year, the number of students enrolled in the four undergraduate courses is 593 students.

Among students of faculty-3 masters of international sports, 19 masters of sports of Uzbekistan, 20 candidates of sports (among them: winners and prize-winners of the Championships of Asia and the Republic of Uzbekistan.

For the implementation of educational programs at the disposal of the faculty there are: classrooms; special sports halls (sports games, gymnastics, martial arts, physical education and a gym for recreation); flat sports grounds in the open air; summer mini-football fields.

Today, TRCHSPI develops more than 10 sports and improves the following sports: Volleyball (men and women), football, wrestling, athletics, chess, checkers, Futsal, handball, basketball (men and women), table tennis.
