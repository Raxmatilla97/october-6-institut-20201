In accordance with the decision No. 1 of the Council of Chirchik state pedagogical Institute dated August 27, 2017, the Department of Russian language began to function at the Faculty of History and languages, separated from the Department of "Languages".  The Department of Russian language is a part of the Faculty of History and languages and trains staff in the following areas:

* Bachelor   " 5111300- Russian language and literature”;

                      “ 5111306-Russian language and literature ( in foreign language groups ) ”

To improve the educational process, teachers of the Department use modern teaching methods, multimedia and information and communication technologies.


 

Students perform practical training in secondary schools of Tashkent region.

 

The Department cooperates with secondary schools and training centers.


International cooperation: today the teaching staff of the Department cooperates with foreign universities and institutes. In particular the Moscow state Institute of Russian language and literature

Teachers of the Department regularly take refresher courses in specialized universities of the country, participate in interregional and international seminars and conferences.
